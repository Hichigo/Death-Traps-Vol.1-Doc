# Relationship Blueprints

## **Traps**

“**BP\_BaseTrap**” - base blueprint for traps, inherited from it:

* “**BP\_BaseMovementTrap**”
* “**BP\_BaseMovementAndRotationTrap**”
* “**BP\_BaseRotationTrap**”
* “**BP\_OpenFloor**” (although it could be inherited from “**BP\_BaseRotationTrap**”).

![](<.gitbook/assets/1 (1).png>)

## **Modules for the traps**

“**BP\_BaseModuleWeapon**” - The base class for a “weapon” trap is what causes damage (axe, spikes, mace, blade, etc.):

* “**BP\_StaticSpike**”
* “**BP\_StaticSpikesOverlap**”
* “**BP\_StaticSpikeHit**”
* “**BP\_SpikesLog**”
* “**BP\_Axe**”
* “**BP\_Blade\_01**”
* “**BP\_Blade\_02**”
